About GreenBoard

Like "pen and paper" Materialize CSS, we present you the 'Chalk and Board' GrenBoard! Developed using Material Framework, the idea behind developing GreenBoard was to offer the user not only the better user-interface, but the best user-experience. It follows the Laws of Designing, that are followed across the globe. However, as every university of India focuses on development and nothing is ever taught related to the designs, Team OSS presents you 'GreenBoard' - the platform which enhances user satisfaction by improving the usability, accessibility and pleasure by providing better interaction with the product.

Still in its Alpha version, GreenBoard will soon cover every possible scheme and laws related to designing, and will soon cover every software module.

Currently optimised for desktop, GreenBoard will soon cover the cross platform hindrances and will be working on small-screen sizes too.
